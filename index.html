Projects

College of Charleston Baseball

Lead Developer • January 2015 - June 2015

Developed a stat tracking and reporting application for The College of Charleston's Division I baseball team. The backend was developed using Django and handled the retrieval of statistics as well as the statistical calculations and aggregations. The backend was designed and implemented with a RESTful API that the front end used to access the stored data. The frontend was powered by AngularJS and generated team level and player by player scouting reports. The reports ranged from traditional text based stat outputs to visual representations of batting spray charts. The application was used by the College of Charleston's coaches and players as apart of their preperation and in game strategy. Reports included lefty/righty splits, batting spray charts, recent performance, and other Sabermetrics not available elsewhere. The 2015 College of Charleston Cougurs won the CAA regular season championship and finished second in the Tallahassee Regional.
